Facts About Sign Language

  • Sign language is a complete, complex language that uses hand gestures, body movements, and facial expressions.
  • There are over 300 different sign languages worldwide.
  • American Sign Language (ASL) is widely used in the United States and Canada.
  • Sign languages are not universal; each has its own grammar and lexicon.
